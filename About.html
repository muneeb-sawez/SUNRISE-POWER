About Sunrise Power

A trusted provider of solar electric solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, committed to promoting clean, sustainable, and renewable energy.

Our Spark: The Story Behind Sunrise Power

Sunrise Power was born from a simple but powerful idea: that every family and business in India deserves reliable, affordable, and clean energy. Living in a region blessed with abundant sunshine, we saw a disconnect between this incredible natural resource and the daily reality of rising electricity bills and frustrating power cuts. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our motivation was to bridge this gap. We envisioned a future where rooftops are not just shelters, but powerhouses of self-reliance. A future where our communities are powered by the sun, reducing our carbon footprint and paving the way for a sustainable tomorrow. This vision is the driving force behind everything we do, from our meticulous installations to our dedicated customer support.
